--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

to play in VLC:
===============
* Media > Open Network Stream...
  * Network URL: http://192.168.XXX.XXX:8080/

to play on Chromecast through VLC:
==================================
* Playback > Renderer
  * displays list of Chromecasts found in LAN
  * select one

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

observations:
=============
* the stream plays perfectly

* "livecamtv.me" video streams need to contact an authorization server at 5 minute intervals
  - otherwise, Streamlink encounters a problem:
